Audit Information

Transparency is a foundational value at GrindBunny. In the spirit of full disclosure, an audit has been conducted by ChatGPT 4.0. While not a traditional audit firm, ChatGPT 4.0 offers an innovative approach that transcends the principal-agent problems often encountered with conventional auditors. For those unfamiliar with the term, it refers to situations where an auditor's interests may not align with those of the public. With the neutrality of ChatGPT 4.0, such concerns are mitigated. Both the token code and the Fundraising Smart Contract code are accessible for scrutiny, and engaging ChatGPT 4.0 (or 3.5) for further inquiries is highly encouraged.

Explain in more detail how the initial distribution will work?

Here's a straightforward example: imagine a hypothetical scenario where the total Ethereum pool amounts to 100 ETH at the end of the contribution period. If Person A has contributed 2 ETH to the pool, their contribution represents 2% of the total pool (2 out of 100). Hence, Person A would be allocated 2%(2.92b) of the 40%(146b) GrindBunny tokens designated for distribution. The more one contributes, the higher the allocation of tokens, adhering to the principle of a fair proportional distribution.
